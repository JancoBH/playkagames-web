2) Information we collect

The information collected depends on how you use the App and which services are enabled (for example, ads).

A) Information stored on your device

We do not require you to create an account. The App may store gameplay progress and settings locally on your device. This data is used to make the App work as expected.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information (such as your name, address, or precise location) through in-app forms.

B) Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads, including optional rewarded ads. AdMob and its partners may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address (for general location estimation and fraud prevention)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, network type)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, reward completion events)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP, where permitted)

This data helps deliver and measure ads, limit fraud, and improve ad performance.

C) Analytics & crash reports (future updates)

In future updates, we may add tools such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand usage, improve stability, and fix crashes. If enabled, these tools may collect:

  • App events (e.g., level start/finish, mode selection)
  • Crash and diagnostic information (to fix errors)
  • Basic device information (model, OS, language)

When these tools are added, we will update this policy and the Google Play Data Safety section accordingly.

3) How we use information

  • Provide the App

    Run core features and store basic settings/progress on-device.

  • Show ads

    Serve interstitial and rewarded ads through AdMob (when online).

  • Prevent fraud & abuse

    Help detect invalid traffic and protect the ad ecosystem.

  • Improve quality

    Fix bugs, prevent abuse, and improve performance over time.

Legal basis / consent: In regions where required (for example, the EEA/UK), we may request your consent before showing personalized ads. You can change ad preferences in your device settings.

5) Your choices

Personalized ads: You may be able to limit ad personalization in your device settings (Android: Google settings → Ads). Availability depends on your region and device.

Rewarded ads are optional: Rewarded ads are shown only if you choose to watch them to receive an in-game bonus.

Offline play: You can play the App offline. Ads require an internet connection.

6) Children’s privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7) Data retention and security

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy. Data handled by third-party providers (such as AdMob) is retained according to their policies.

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8) International data transfers

Third-party services we use may process information on servers located in different countries. By using the App, you understand that information may be processed outside your country of residence.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
