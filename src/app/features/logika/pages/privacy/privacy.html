Logika — Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

1) Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Playka Games ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Logika (the “App”).

2) Information we collect

The information collected depends on how you use the App and which services are enabled (for example, ads).

On-device data (local storage): The App may store gameplay progress and settings locally on your device. We do not require account creation.
Advertising (Google AdMob): We use Google AdMob to display ads, including optional rewarded ads. Ad services may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), IP address, device information (model/OS/language/network), ad interaction data, and approximate location (derived from IP, where permitted).
Analytics & crash reports (future updates): In future updates, we may add tools such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand usage and improve stability. If enabled, we will update this policy and the Google Play Data Safety section accordingly.

Note: We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information (such as your name, address, or precise location) through in-app forms.

3) How we use information

We use information to operate the App and improve gameplay:

  • Provide the App: Run core features and store basic progress/settings on your device.
  • Show ads: Serve interstitial and rewarded ads through AdMob (when online).
  • Prevent fraud & abuse: Help detect invalid traffic and protect the ad ecosystem.
  • Improve quality: Fix bugs, improve performance, and enhance features over time.

Legal basis / consent: In regions where required (for example, the EEA/UK), consent may be requested before showing personalized ads. You can change ad preferences in your device settings.

4) Offline play

Logika can be played offline. Ads and rewarded ads require an internet connection and may not be available offline.

5) Sharing and third parties

We may share information with third-party service providers that help operate the App, such as advertising providers.

Google AdMob: Used to serve and measure ads, including rewarded ads.
Other services (future): We may add analytics or crash reporting in future updates; if we do, we will update this policy.

Third parties process data under their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information.

6) Your choices

  • Personalized ads: You may be able to limit ad personalization in your device settings (Android: Google settings → Ads). Availability depends on your region and device.
  • Rewarded ads are optional: Rewarded ads are shown only if you choose to watch them to receive an in-game bonus.
  • Offline play: You can play offline; ads require an internet connection.

7) Children’s privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8) Data retention and security

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy. Data handled by third-party providers (such as AdMob) is retained according to their policies.

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) International data transfers

Third-party services we use may process information on servers located in different countries. By using the App, you understand that information may be processed outside your country of residence.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date on this page. Your continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
