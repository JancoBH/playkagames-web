FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

? How do I play Logika?

Logika is a collection of puzzle mini-games. Each mini-game has 10 levels, and each level includes 4 to 6 puzzles. Tap to interact and solve them before time runs out in Normal Mode, or take your time in Free Mode.

? What is Normal Mode?

Normal Mode is the main challenge mode where you need to complete all puzzles before the timer runs out. Completing levels in Normal Mode earns you puzzle pieces for your collection.

? What is Free Mode?

Free Mode lets you play without time pressure. It’s perfect for practicing or relaxing. It includes level-based play and a Random Mode for quick, endless sessions.

? What is Random Mode?

Random Mode is part of Free Mode. Puzzles are generated randomly without following specific levels. Great for short sessions when you just want to solve puzzles.

? How do I earn puzzle pieces?

You earn 1 puzzle piece every time you complete a level in Normal Mode. Logika includes 20+ collectibles with 100+ pieces to collect as you progress.

? Do I need internet to play?

No. You can play Logika offline. An internet connection is only required for ads—especially rewarded ads (optional) if you choose to watch them.

? How many puzzles are there?

Logika features 500+ puzzles across 10+ mini-games, with more content added over time. Each mini-game focuses on different skills like logic, memory, and problem-solving.

? Is Logika free to play?

Yes, Logika is free to download and play. Enjoy all puzzle content without any paywalls.
